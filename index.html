How to Use

Integrating Dunno into your business phone system is a straightforward process that can provide significant benefits to your customers and your bottom line. Here are the steps to follow for a successful integration.

  • Sign up for Dunno's service and receive API credentials.
  • Configure the company's phone system to forward calls to Dunno's API endpoint.
  • Map the phone system's menu options to the relevant Dunno intents and entities.
  • Train the Dunno models on the company's specific customer service data to ensure accurate responses.
  • Test the integration and refine as necessary.
  • Launch the integrated system and monitor performance to ensure customer satisfaction.

Services We Provide

Dunno provides an innovative solution for businesses to effectively manage high volume call traffic and improve customer satisfaction through our AI-powered conversational system. Our platform allows businesses to automate their customer service process, reducing call wait times and improving first-call resolution rates. With Dunno, businesses can save costs on call center staffing and increase revenue through improved customer retention and acquisition.

  • 01 Customer Feedback

    Dunno provides customer feedback as a service by analyzing and categorizing customer queries and complaints. This allows businesses to understand their customers' needs and concerns, and make informed decisions to improve their products and services.

  • 02 Custmer Queries

    As an AI-powered platform, Dunno can effectively handle customer queries through natural language processing and machine learning algorithms. This enables the platform to understand and interpret customer questions and provide accurate and helpful responses in real-time. With Dunno, businesses can improve their customer service and reduce the workload on their support staff by automating the handling of common queries. Additionally, the platform can provide insights into customer queries, allowing businesses to identify trends and areas where they may need to improve their products or services.

  • 03 Order Tracking

    Dunno provides an efficient and user-friendly order tracking service, allowing customers to easily check the status of their orders and get real-time updates on their delivery. With our advanced technology and trained models, we ensure accurate and up-to-date information is provided to customers, reducing the need for manual customer support and enhancing the overall customer experience.
